# Personal Record brand profile

Status: product direction. The name and mark require trademark and domain clearance before public launch.

## Core identity

- Customer name: **Personal Record**
- Short name: **Record**
- Repository name: **Personal Server**
- Promise: **Your records, kept useful.**
- Category: a personal record service, not a generic life dashboard
- Product metaphor: a suite of well-kept personal instruments sharing one ledger

Record turns daily source rows into something easy to log, inspect, correct, and review. It should feel private, precise, calm, and durable. It must never imply that AI generated a fact that came from a user, import, bank notification, health permission, or connected service.

## Voice

Write like a careful record keeper: direct, specific, and unhurried.

- Prefer “3 sets remain” to “Crush your goals.”
- Prefer “Detected from a Revolut notification” to “Smart transaction.”
- Prefer “Last verified 4 minutes ago” to “Synced.”
- Prefer “Series” and “Cash” in customer navigation; retain internal route and database names in code.
- Explain consequences before destructive or permission-sensitive actions.

Avoid hype, faux intimacy, productivity guilt, “AI-powered” as a substitute for behavior, unsupported security claims, and invented metrics.

## Visual system

The base world is blue-black graphite with cool hairlines, precise typography, and restrained depth. Domain colors identify instruments without repainting whole pages.

| Role | Direction |

| Panel | graphite `#111a27` |

Use Sora Variable for the interface and JetBrains Mono Variable for amounts, sets, progress, time, ranks, dates, and source state. Both fonts are self-hosted for reliable web, PWA, and native rendering.

## Mark

The mark is a modular constellation: seven domain nodes connected around one personal center. Use the complete mark, preserve a clear space equal to one quarter of its width, and keep it legible as a single-color mask at small sizes. A restrained blue-to-ultraviolet treatment is reserved for identity moments; navigation uses the single-color form.

Use **Personal Record** in customer product surfaces. Use **Personal Server** only when referring to the repository, deployment package, or self-host operations.

## Interface grammar

- Records, tables, timelines, and purpose-built work surfaces before floating card walls.
- Source records before aggregate claims.
- One dominant action per working surface.
- Persistent global navigation; local navigation never replaces it.
- Desktop is the review and correction workspace. Mobile is the fast capture and continuation surface.
- Status is text plus shape or position, never color alone.
- Motion confirms cause and effect in roughly 120–260 ms. Respect reduced motion.
- Empty states explain the first useful action; they do not reserve theatrical space.

## Imagery

Show the actual interface and recognizable source artifacts: a workout set, a transaction row, a series progress line, an album cover, or a habit decision. Avoid stock lifestyle photography, abstract AI meshes, fake dashboards, floating glass cards, and fabricated customer data presented as real activity.

## Brand portfolio

- **Personal Record / Record**: customer application and managed service.
- **Personal Server**: public source repository and self-host distribution.
- **Record for Households**: reserved future plan; do not launch until permissions and shared/private record boundaries are designed.
- Domain names such as Gym, Cash, Series, Habits, and Spotify are product instruments, not independent sub-brands.

## Launch gate

Before public naming or paid acquisition: complete EU/US trademark screening, domain and app-store-name checks, social handle review, and counsel approval. If clearance fails, preserve the identity system and replace only the customer name and wordmark.
